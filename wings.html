Traditional Buffalo Chicken Wings

Prep Time:

10 mins

Cook Time:

10 mins

Additional Time:

1 hrs

Total Time:

1 hrs 20 minutes

Servings:

5

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground paptrika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 10 chicken wings
  • 2 cups vegatable oil for frying, or as needed
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
  • 1 pinch garlic powder

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat; stir in flour until smooth. Add onion; cook and stir until onion is softened, 5 to 10 minutes. Add black olives, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon oil, paprika, black pepper, and salt; cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes. Stir in chicken; remove the skillet from heat and cover.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat oil in a large, heavy saucepan or deep fryer over medium-high heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Fill tortillas with chicken mixture. Roll tortilla around filling and secure with a toothpick.

  4. Step 4

    5. Working in batches, fry rolled tortillas in hot oil until browned, about 2 minutes. Use a slotted spoon or tongs to transfer tortillas to a paper towel-lined plate; serve warm.
